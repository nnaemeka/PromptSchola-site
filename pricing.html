Questions you might have

Do I need a credit card to use the free plan?

No. You can explore the free steps for all topics without entering any payment information.

What exactly does “Steps 1–3 free” mean?

For every topic (for example Newton’s First Law), you can use the Orient, Understand, and Apply prompts for free. Steps 4–7 (examples, summaries, practice, experiment) are part of Mastery Access.

Does one subscription cover all subjects?

Yes. Mastery Access covers all active subjects in PromptSchola: Physics first, then Math, Chemistry, Biology, and Earth Science as they are added.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. You can cancel your Mastery subscription at any time. You’ll keep access until the end of your billing period.
