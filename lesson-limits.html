Limits & Continuity

Limits are the foundation of calculus. They describe what value a function is approaching, even when it doesn’t actually reach that value. Continuity captures when a function behaves “smoothly” with no jumps or holes. Use the prompts below to build intuition step by step.

Suggested background: algebra & basic functions · Typical course: Calculus I
