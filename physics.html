How Physics works in PromptSchola

Each topic (like Newton’s Laws or Electric Fields) is a small journey through the 7 steps: Orient → Understand → Apply → Work Through It → Summary → Practice → Experiment.

  • • Start with a quick orientation and diagnostic check.
  • • Build intuition with simple explanations and examples.
  • • See how the idea shows up in engineering, science, and everyday life.
  • • Then dive into solved problems, practice sets, and simulations when you’re ready.
